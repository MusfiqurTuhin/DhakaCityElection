Executive Summary

Administrative Evolution

Dhaka city has experienced four major administrative transformations since its municipal inception. Established as Dhaka Municipality (Aug 1, 1864), it was transformed into Dhaka Pour Corporation on September 30, 1983, renamed Dhaka City Corporation (DCC) in 1990, and finally bifurcated in 2011 into Dhaka North City Corporation (DNCC) and Dhaka South City Corporation (DSCC).
