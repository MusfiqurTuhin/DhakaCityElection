Key Takeaways

Slower, Denser Growth

The population is still growing, but the annual growth rate has slowed significantly. The district has become much denser, packing more people into the same area.

Improving Living Standards

There's been a major jump in literacy (+14 points) and a significant shift from semi-permanent to permanent 'Pucca' housing, showing a clear quality of life improvement.

A Tale of Two Cities

DSCC is the denser, older core with slightly better basic sanitation. DNCC, while having more slums, leads in tech adoption, financial inclusion, and female mobile use.
